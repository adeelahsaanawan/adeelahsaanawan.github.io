Adeel Ahsan

I am a MEng Student at ISL at MUN, where I am being advised by Dr. George Mann and Dr. Oscar De Silva, supported by a fully funded scholarship. I have a bachelor's degree in aerospace engineering from IST.

My research develops optimal guidance and control algorithms for aerospace systems, including drones and helicopters. I have worked on implementing nonlinear model predictive control, incremental nonlinear dynamic inversion control, and nonlinear active disturbance rejection control on a quadrotor.

Outside of research, I enjoy traveling, playing football, hitting the gym, and creating content.
